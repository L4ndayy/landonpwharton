I grew up in Livonia, MI; and currently reside in Green Oak, MI. I have attended three public schools in my lifetime, which are the following: Brummer Elementary School, Millennium Middle School, and am enrolled in South Lyon High School. I am Catholic, and I am an active parish member at St. Patrick Catholic Parish located in Brighton Michigan. I attend Sunday mass as well as Sunday and Wednesday evening Young Church. Next year, I will be attending a four-year college/university, which I will update this page with as soon as I decide which school I will be attending this fall.

Some as listed are coding in the HTML and CSS language, and graphic design. On my projects page, you will see a link that directs you to a flyer I made for my high school swim team.
